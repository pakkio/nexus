Name: Boros
Area: Mountain
Role: Retired Warrior / Goatherd, Witness to a Past Veil Breach
Motivation: Live peacefully and pass on wisdom & warnings about the Veil.
Goal: Teach a worthy soul and ensure his warnings are heeded before he dies.
Needed Object: Lost Goat Bell (a test of worthiness)
Treasure: Testimonianza Incisa di Boros (Warrior's Keepsake containing a detailed account of a past Veil breach)
Veil Connection: Survived a Veil breach; remembers truths and horrors no one else dares speak. His memories are a direct proof of the Veil's fragility.
PlayerHint: Boros ha visto cose terribili. Guadagna la sua fiducia ritrovando la sua campanella. La sua testimonianza è una prova cruciale per Lyra.
Dialogue Hooks:
- "The wind up here doesn’t lie. Listen to it next time... sussurra di antichi orrori."
- "I buried my sword beneath the oldest goat. Don’t ask how I know which one. Ma ho seppellito anche ricordi più oscuri."
- "Questa incisione... che possa servire da monito. Il Velo si è già squarciato una volta."
