Name: The Shattered Veil
Description:
For centuries, the world of Eldoria has been protected by the Veil, a barrier of mystical energy separating the material realm from the chaotic magic beyond. Now the Veil is fraying, and strange anomalies ripple across the land. Each NPC holds a clue, a key, or a curse connected to this unraveling.

Themes:
- Forgotten truths
- Leaking magic
- Fractured dreams!
