Name: Jorin
Area: Tavern
Role: Tavern Keeper, Collector of Whispers and Fractured Dreams
Motivation: Stay informed and safe, trading secrets as currency.
Goal: Collect secrets and chronicle the strange occurrences tied to the Veil's decay.
Needed Object: Rare Coin (as payment for his information/collection)
Treasure: Diario dei Sogni Infranti (Mystic Token is its key/cover; contains notes on anomalous dreams reflecting Veil tears)
Veil Connection: Tracks dreams from travelers that are eerily identical; keeps a book of them. These shared nightmares are a symptom of the Veil fraying the collective unconscious.
PlayerHint: Jorin ascolta più di quanto dia a vedere. Una Moneta Rara potrebbe convincerlo a condividere il suo "Diario dei Sogni Infranti", un'altra prova per Lyra o un componente per un Sigillo.
Dialogue Hooks:
- "You can learn more from a drunk’s mumble than a priest’s sermon... specialmente di questi tempi."
- "Funny thing, someone matching your look passed through here last night… paid in foreign coin... e parlava di sogni strani."
- "Found this odd Mystic Token the other day cleaning up. Not sure what it's for. Do you want it? Potrebbe aprire qualcosa di... interessante."
- "Questo diario... la gente sogna cose che non dovrebbe. Il Velo si sta logorando anche nelle loro menti."
